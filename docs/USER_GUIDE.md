# Listo User Guide

Listo is a smart list app for groceries, tasks, and everyday planning. It works
in your browser, can be installed like a native app, keeps working offline, and
lets you share lists with family and friends in real time.

This guide covers everything you can do in the app today.

---

## 1. Getting started

### Sign in

Open Listo and create an account with **email and password**, or sign in with
**Google** or **Apple**. If you forget your password, use the reset link on the
login screen — you'll get an email that takes you to a password-recovery page.

> **Guest access:** you don't need an account to join a list someone shared
> with you. Opening their invite link offers a guest sign-in — just pick a
> display name and you're in. Guests can later upgrade to a full account to
> keep their lists.

### Install the app (optional)

Listo is a Progressive Web App. When your browser supports it, an install
banner appears — accept it to add Listo to your home screen or dock. Installed
or not, the app shell and your data are cached, so Listo opens and works even
without a connection (see [Offline](#9-offline)).

### Finding your way around

The bottom navigation has three parts:

- **Lists** — your home screen: every list you own or joined
- **＋** (center button) — create a new list
- **Profile** — your account, preferences, and settings

## 2. Creating lists

Tap the center **＋** button. Give the list a name, pick a **type**, and
optionally choose an icon:

| Type | Best for |
|---|---|
| **Shopping** | Groceries and shopping runs — unlocks Shop Mode, Insights, and Before You Go |
| **Tasks** | To-dos and chores |
| **Personal** | Anything else — packing lists, wishlists, ideas |

You can start from a **blank list** or from one of **your templates**
(see [Templates](#7-templates-duplicating-and-archiving)).

### The Lists screen

Your lists are grouped by filter chips at the top: **Active**, **Shared**,
**Completed**, and **Archived**. You can also:

- **Search** by list name, member name, or category
- **Sort** by last activity, name, and more
- **Swipe a list card left** for quick actions — including **Archive**

---

## 3. Adding items

Open a list and tap **Add item**. Listo's smart input does the heavy lifting:

### Smart input

Type the item and quantity together — there's no separate quantity field:

```
Milk ×2
Rice 2kg
Eggs 12
```

Listo splits this into the item name, quantity, and unit automatically.

### Auto-categorization

Items are categorized as you add them (`Milk → Dairy`, `Rice → Pantry`),
using your own category set — see [Categories](#10-categories).

### Your regulars & type-ahead

Listo learns what you add over time (privately, per user):

- **Your regulars** — your frequently used items are suggested right in the
  add sheet, one tap to add
- **Type-ahead** — start typing and Listo suggests matching items along with
  the quantity you usually buy

### Duplicate detection

If you type something that looks like it's already on the list, Listo flags it
and asks whether to **Merge Qty** (combine the quantities into the existing
item) or **Add Separate**. It never blocks you — you decide.

## 4. Working with a list

- **Tap an item** to check it off; completed items move to their own section
  and record who completed them and when
- **Swipe an item** for quick actions like edit and delete
- **Sort** items A→Z, by category, or by date added (List Options → Sort)
- **Customize List View** (List Options) — choose what shows on each item:
  categories, quantity, who added it, and whether completed items stay visible
- A **progress bar** at the top tracks how much of the list is done

### List Options menu

The **⋯** menu in the list header adapts to the list — you'll only see actions
that apply right now:

- **View & Tools** — Sort · Insights (shopping lists) · Before You Go
  (shopping lists, when you have forgotten regulars) · Customize List View
- **Manage List** — Rename · Duplicate · Save as Template · Share
  (the last three are owner-only)
- **Reports** — Export Report (once the list has items)
- **Cleanup** — Clear Completed (permanently removes checked-off items,
  with confirmation)
- **Danger Zone** — Delete List (owner-only, with confirmation)

## 5. Shopping features

These are available on **shopping** lists.

### Shop Mode

Start Shop Mode from the list header when you head to the store. It's a
focused, full-screen checklist for the shopping run — check items off as they
go in the cart, and when everything's in, tap **Finish Shopping**. The
completion card celebrates the run and offers **Start new list** to reset the
list for your next trip — either **keeping all items**, keeping **just your
regulars**, or starting from an **empty list**.

### Before You Go

Before you leave, Listo compares the list against your regulars and points out
items you usually buy but haven't added — one tap adds them. It appears in the
List Options menu whenever there's something to suggest.

### Insights

Open **Insights** from the List Options menu for per-list shopping analytics:

- **Shopping Health** — an overall planning score, compared with your
  previous lists, with concrete recommendations to improve it
- **Shopping Efficiency** — completion rate, average items per list
- **Shopping Behaviour** — your most active day, top adder, top completer,
  and member activity on shared lists
- **Category Analysis** — how your items break down by category
- **Predicted Next List** — once you have enough history, Listo predicts
  your next shopping list and can create it for you in one tap

Insights are per-list — open them from the list you're interested in.

## 6. Sharing & collaborating

### Invite someone

As the list owner, open **Share** (from the List Options menu or the list
header). Listo creates a private **invite link** you can copy or send via
Messages, WhatsApp, or anything on your share sheet.

Before sharing, choose what the link grants:

- **Can edit** — invitees become collaborators who can add, check off, and
  edit items
- **View only** — invitees can see the list but not change it

The person you invite just opens the link — they can sign in, create an
account, or join as a **guest** with only a display name. The join page shows
them the list name, owner, and member count before they commit.

### Managing access

- Links are **multi-use** — one link can bring in the whole family
- **Reset the link** anytime (Share sheet) — older links immediately stop
  working, and switching between Can edit / View only also resets it
- Owners can change a member's role or **remove a member** from the members
  view
- Joining is *only* possible via the invite link — there's no way to look up
  or guess your way into someone's list

### Real-time sync

Shared lists update live: when someone adds or checks off an item, everyone
sees it within moments. Items show **who added** and **who completed** them,
so it's always clear what happened.

## 7. Templates, duplicating, and archiving

- **Save as Template** (List Options, owner-only) turns a list into a reusable
  template — great for the monthly grocery run or a packing list. Your
  templates appear when creating a new list.
- **Duplicate** makes an immediate one-off copy of a list.
- **Archive** a finished list by swiping its card on the Lists screen — it
  moves under the **Archived** chip, out of the way but never deleted. Swipe
  an archived card to **Restore** it (or delete it for good).

---

## 8. Reports

**Export Report** (List Options) generates a shareable summary of the list —
items, quantities, categories, members, and completion details — as a **PDF**
or **CSV**. On phones it opens the native share sheet; elsewhere it downloads
the file.

---

## 9. Offline

Listo is built to survive spotty connections:

- The app opens and your lists are readable with no connection at all
- **Adding, editing, checking off, and deleting items works offline** — your
  changes apply instantly on your device, are saved locally, and sync
  automatically in order when you're back online
- List-level actions (creating, renaming, sharing, archiving, deleting lists)
  need a connection

---

## 10. Categories

Categories are yours to shape. Go to **Profile → Categories** to rename,
merge, add, or remove categories per list type. Auto-categorization and the
category filter/sort use *your* set everywhere — on every device you sign
into.

---

## 11. Profile & settings

The **Profile** tab collects everything about you and the app:

- **Account** — change your password, or delete your account and all its data
- **Preferences** — **Theme** (light / dark / follow system) and your
  **default list type** for new lists
- **Collaboration** — set the display name other members see on shared lists
- **Invite** — share Listo itself with friends
- **Help & Support / About** — get help, read the Terms and Privacy Policy,
  and see app info
- **Sign out**

## Quick FAQ

**Can someone join my list without an invite link?**
No. The link is the only way in, and you can reset it at any time.

**What happens to items I check off?**
They move to the completed section with who/when details. **Clear Completed**
removes them permanently; otherwise they stay for reference and feed your
shopping insights.

**Do guests have accounts?**
Guests are anonymous sessions with a display name. They can participate fully
in lists they've joined, and can create a real account later.

**Why don't I see Insights / Shop Mode / Before You Go on my list?**
They're specific to **shopping** lists. Before You Go additionally appears
only when Listo has regulars to suggest.

**Is my data shared with other users?**
Only lists you explicitly share. Your item history ("your regulars"),
categories, and insights are private to your account.
